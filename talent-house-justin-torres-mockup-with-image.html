Why He Fights

Justin Torres is an 18-year-old amateur fighter from Biloxi, Mississippi. He lives with his single mother and younger sister, and every decision he makes is about creating opportunity for his family.

Boxing gave him discipline and focus. MMA gives him a bigger stage. Outside the gym, Justin cooks for his family and spends time fixing up an old truck — learning patience the same way he learned how to fight.

“I’m not chasing shortcuts. I’m building something for my family.”
